# FORBIDDEN Commands

NEVER execute these. No exceptions - not with permission, user request, or plan approval.

**Git:** `reset`, `clean`, `checkout --`/`restore` (discard), `stash drop/clear`, `branch -D`, `push --force`, `rebase`, `merge/rebase --abort`, `reflog expire`, `gc --prune`

**Files:** `rm -rf`, `rm -r`, `rm` with wildcards, `rmdir` non-empty, `del /s`, `rd /s`

**Database:** `DROP`, `TRUNCATE`, `DELETE` without WHERE
